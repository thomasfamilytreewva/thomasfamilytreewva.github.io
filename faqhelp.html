What is the Thomas Family Tree Website?

The Thomas Family Tree Website in an online presentation of the Thomas family

The primary goal is to honor family members past, present and future



What are some of the highlights of the Thomas Family Tree Website?

---A geneological family tree that starts with Joe Thomas' grandmother (Chaney)

and spans 8 generations

---Some original pages from the William Henry Thomas (Joe Thomas' father) bible

---Search features that lets users find any Thomas family member over the last

8 generations.



How do I use Thomas Family Tree Website?

It's best viewed on a PC or Laptop but can also be viewed on mobile devices

It's simple design allows users to explore the Thomas family

based on the users interests



How is the Thomas Family Tree website funded.

It's a labor of love and all costs are covered by the grace of God
